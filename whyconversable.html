Christine Raine

Founding Director

mail@mail.com

Shanti has worked in the field of education for over 25 years. In Cape Verde, Africa she served as a link between the government and rural communities in order to promote development projects and cooperatives. She was also a professor and Faculty Advisor for the Global Studies Program in Costa Rica, run by Long Island University. Shanti specialized in Peace Education and had an active role as a volunteer for the Peace Academy of Costa Rica, where she participated documenting the initial of their programs at public schools, as a trainer and evaluator. This experience fired up her passion for empathic communication as a powerful social transformation tool. She feels ecstatic to be able to contribute with these tools once again, from ConversABLE.
